    Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideals for portfolio building.

  • Is Frontend Mentor free?

    Yes and no. It's Freemium. There's free challenges like this one and other challenges you have to pay for.

    I don't see why not?
